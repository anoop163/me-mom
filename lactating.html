DO's and DONT's for Lactating Mother

    DO's :-

    • Balanced Diet
    • Consume lots of protien
    • Early initiation of breast feeding is must(within one hour after delivery)
    • Drink plenty of water
    • 0-6 months exclusive breast feeding

    DONT's :-

    • Avoid stress as it lowers the production of Milk
    • Avoid Smoking
    • Avoid Consumption of Alcohol
