DO's and DONT's during pregnancy

    DO's :-

    • Balanced Diet
    • Intake of folic acids and Calcium tablets for 180 days
    • Morning Sleep required is 2 hours
    • Night sleep required is 8-10 hours
    • Regular checkups

    DONT's :-

    • Smoking should be avoided
    • Consumption Of Alcohol should be avoided
