DO's and DONT's for 3-5 years old kids

    DO's :-

    • Include all sorts of breakfast dishes like idli, dosa, upma, chapathi etc
    • Habituate too family food and include required essential nutrients
    • Include green veggies in their diet
    • Include fish, chicken and other non-veg dishes(if non-vegetarian)

    DONT's :-

    • Avoid junkfoods like burger, pizza etc
    • Stop giving breastmilk after 3rd year
